Our Mission

"Making student housing search simple, transparent, and stress-free"

At JoouStay, we understand that finding the right accommodation as a student can be overwhelming. Between managing coursework, adjusting to campus life, and maintaining a budget, the last thing you need is housing stress. That's why we created a platform that puts students first, connecting you with verified, affordable housing options within walking distance or a short commute to your school.
