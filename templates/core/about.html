Our Mission

"Making student housing search at JOOUST simple, transparent, and stress-free"

At JoouStay, we know how challenging it can be for JOOUST students to find a good place to stay. Between classes, tight budgets, and adjusting to campus life, housing shouldn’t be another burden. That’s why our platform focuses only on JOOUST, helping students find vacant houses close to campus and, if needed, match with roommates to make rent more affordable.

Why Choose JoouStay?

We built JoouStay for JOOUST students — focusing on nearby housing, affordable options, and practical roommate connections.

Vacant House Search

Easily browse vacant houses around JOOUST and request more details. We make it simple to find housing that fits your needs and budget.

Verified Listings

Our team checks listings to make sure the houses posted are real and available, so you don’t waste time chasing false leads.

Fair Pricing

Students pay rent to the landlord and a small service fee to JoouStay for making the process easier. No hidden charges.

Close to Campus

All houses listed on JoouStay are within reach of JOOUST’s main campus, so you can walk or take a short ride to class.

Roommate Search

Need a roommate? Use our platform to connect with other JOOUST students looking to share housing, cut costs, and build community.

Student Support

Our team is available to answer questions, arrange viewings, and help you if you need to cancel or change your choice.
