Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about JoouStay and how we help students rent houses around JOOUST campus.

How do I rent a house?

Search for vacant houses on our platform and fill out a request form for the place you like. Our team will then connect you with the landlord, share more details, or even take you to view the house before you decide to rent.

What fees do I pay?

You'll pay rent to the landlord, plus a small service fee to JoouStay for helping you secure housing and guiding the process.

Can I view a house before renting?

Yes. After you submit a request, you can schedule a viewing before making any payments. We only process payments once you decide to rent.

What if I want to cancel after choosing a house?

If you need to cancel, contact our support team by call or email. We'll assist you with the cancellation and explain any conditions that may apply.
