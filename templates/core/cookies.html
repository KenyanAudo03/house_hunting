What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device when you visit our website. They help us provide you with a better experience by remembering your preferences and improving how our platform works for you.

JoouStay uses cookies to enhance your experience when searching for housing near JOOUST campus and connecting with potential roommates.

How We Use Cookies

We use cookies to:

  • Keep you logged in to your account
  • Remember your search preferences and filters
  • Improve our website's performance and functionality
  • Understand how students use our platform
  • Provide personalized housing recommendations
  • Ensure the security of your account

Types of Cookies We Use

JoouStay uses different types of cookies for various purposes:

Cookie Categories

We categorize our cookies based on their function and how long they remain on your device.

Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the basic functioning of JoouStay. They enable core features like:

  • User authentication and login sessions
  • Security features to protect your account
  • Basic website navigation and functionality
  • Form submissions for housing inquiries

Essential cookies cannot be disabled as they are required for the platform to work properly. They are automatically deleted when you close your browser or after your session expires.

Analytics Cookies

We use analytics cookies to understand how students interact with JoouStay. These cookies help us:

  • Track which housing listings are most popular
  • Understand user navigation patterns
  • Identify areas for platform improvement
  • Monitor website performance and load times
  • Analyze search behavior to improve results

Analytics cookies collect anonymous information and cannot identify you personally. This data helps us make JoouStay more useful for JOOUST students.

Functionality Cookies

These cookies enhance your experience on JoouStay by remembering your choices and preferences:

  • Your preferred search filters (price range, number of bedrooms)
  • Recently viewed housing listings
  • Saved housing searches
  • Roommate matching preferences

Functionality cookies make using JoouStay more convenient by personalizing your experience based on your housing needs and preferences.

Managing Cookies

Browser Settings

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to:

  • View which cookies are stored on your device
  • Delete existing cookies
  • Block cookies from specific websites
  • Block all cookies (not recommended for JoouStay)

Impact of Disabling Cookies

Important Notice

Disabling cookies may affect your ability to use certain features of JoouStay, including staying logged in, saving search preferences, and receiving personalized housing recommendations.

Updates to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons.

When we make significant changes, we will notify users by updating the "Updated" date at the top of this page and may provide additional notice through our platform or email.

We encourage you to review this Cookie Policy periodically to stay informed about how we use cookies to improve your JoouStay experience.
