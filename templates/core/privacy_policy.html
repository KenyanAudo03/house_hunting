Introduction

In our mission to make student housing better for everyone at JOOUST, we collect and use information about you, our

  • students using JoouStay to find housing and roommates
  • property owners listing their houses on our platform
  • visitors browsing our website and services

This Privacy Policy will help you better understand how we collect, use, and share your personal information. If we change our privacy practices, we will post those changes on this page and update the revision date. If the changes are significant, we will provide a more prominent notice or get your consent as required by law.

Our values

Trust is the foundation of the JoouStay platform and includes trusting us to do the right thing with your information. Three main values guide us as we develop our products and services. These values should help you better understand how we think about your information and privacy.

Your information belongs to you

We carefully analyze what types of information we need to provide our services, and we try to limit the information we collect to only what we really need. Where possible, we delete or anonymize this information when we no longer need it. When building and improving our products, our engineers work closely with our privacy and security teams to build in privacy by design.

We protect your information from others

If a third party requests your personal information, we will refuse to share it unless you give us permission or we are legally required to do so. When we are legally required to share your personal information, we will tell you before we do so, unless we are legally forbidden.

We help students make informed decisions about their housing

We will never sell your personal information to anyone. We use your information to provide better housing recommendations and help you connect with suitable roommates, but we are thoughtful about how we do so.

Information we collect

Information you provide to us

We collect information you provide directly to us, such as when you create or modify your account, request student housing services, contact us for support, or otherwise communicate with us. This information may include:

  • Name, email address, phone number, and other contact information
  • Housing preferences, budget range, and lifestyle information
  • Messages and communications with other users and our support team
  • Payment and billing information for service fees
  • Any other information you choose to provide

Information we collect automatically

When you access or use our services, we automatically collect information about you, including:

  • Log Information: We log information about your use of our services, including access times, pages viewed, IP address, and browser type
  • Device Information: We collect information about the device you use to access our services, including hardware model, operating system, and mobile network information
  • Location Information: With your consent, we may collect information about the location of your device to show nearby housing options
  • Usage Information: We collect information relating to your use of our services, including search queries and interaction with listings

Student verification is important

We verify that you're a JOOUST student to maintain the safety and integrity of our housing community. This helps ensure our platform serves only verified university students.

How we use your information

We use information about you for various purposes, including to:

  • Provide, operate, and maintain our housing platform services
  • Match you with suitable housing options and roommates near JOOUST
  • Process transactions and send related information, including confirmations and receipts
  • Send technical notices, updates, security alerts, and support messages
  • Respond to your comments, questions, and provide customer service
  • Communicate with you about products, services, and events
  • Monitor and analyze trends, usage, and activities in connection with our services
  • Detect, investigate, and prevent fraudulent transactions and other illegal activities
  • Personalize and improve our services and provide content or features that match user profiles

Sharing your information

We may share information about you as follows or as otherwise described in this Privacy Policy:

With other users

When you use our roommate matching service, certain profile information may be shared with other students to facilitate housing connections.

With property owners

When you express interest in a property, we share your contact information with property owners to enable direct communication.

With vendors, consultants, and other service providers

We may share your information with third parties who need access to such information to carry out work on our behalf, including payment processing, email delivery, and customer support.

In response to legal requests

If we receive a subpoena or other legal request, we may need to inspect the data we hold to determine how to respond.

With your consent

We may also share personal information with your consent or at your direction.

We will never sell your personal information

We have never sold personal information about our users and we never will. We're not a data broker, we're a housing platform for students.

Your rights

You have certain rights with respect to your personal information, including:

  • Access: You can request access to your personal information and receive information about how we process it
  • Correction: If your personal information is inaccurate or incomplete, you can request that we correct it
  • Deletion: You can request that we delete your personal information
  • Portability: You can request a copy of your personal information in a structured, machine-readable format
  • Objection: You can object to our processing of your personal information under certain circumstances

To exercise these rights, please contact us at privacy@jooustay.com. We will respond to your request within 30 days.

Data security

We take reasonable measures to help protect personal information from loss, theft, misuse, and unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, and destruction. However, no internet or electronic storage system is 100% secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Cookies and tracking

We use various technologies to collect information, including cookies and web beacons. Cookies are small data files stored on your device that help us improve our services and your experience.

Data retention

We store the information we collect about you for as long as is necessary for the purpose(s) for which we originally collected it. We may retain certain information for legitimate business purposes or as required by law.

Changes to this policy

We may change this privacy policy from time to time. If we make changes, we will notify you by revising the date at the top of the policy and, in some cases, provide you with additional notice.
