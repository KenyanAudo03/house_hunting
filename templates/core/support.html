Frequently Asked Questions

Finding Housing

How do I search for available houses near JOOUST?

Use the search bar to filter by name, location, price, or type. The system will show matching listings available near JOOUST campus.

Are all the house listings verified?

Yes! JoouStay gathers and verifies all property information before listing. Our team checks that houses are real, available, and accurately described to save you time and prevent disappointment.

How do I request to view a house?

Click the provided phone or WhatsApp link on a listing to contact us directly, or fill out the contact form and our team will get in touch with you regarding the property.

Can I save houses I'm interested in?

Yes! Create an account to save your favorite listings and receive updates when similar houses become available.

Roommate Matching

How does roommate matching work?

Create a roommate profile with your preferences, budget, and contact information. Other JOOUST students can view your profile and contact you directly if you seem like a good match.

Is roommate matching only for JOOUST students?

Yes, JoouStay is exclusively for JOOUST students. This ensures all potential roommates are part of the same campus community.

How do I contact potential roommates?

Roommate profiles include contact information provided by the user. You can reach out to them directly using their preferred contact method.

Can I update my roommate preferences?

Absolutely! Log into your account anytime to update your roommate profile, preferences, or contact information.

Account Issues

I forgot my password. How do I reset it?

Click "Forgot Password" on the login page and enter your email address. We'll send you a link to create a new password.

How do I verify my JOOUST student status?

During registration, you'll need to provide your email. We verify this information to ensure platform security.

Can I change my email address?

Yes, go to your account settings to update your email address. You'll need to verify the new email before the change takes effect.

How do I delete my account?

Contact our support team to request account deletion. We'll remove your information while keeping necessary records for legal compliance.

Payments & Fees

What fees does JoouStay charge?

JoouStay charges a small service fee for facilitating housing connections. You pay rent directly to the property owner. All fees are clearly shown before you agree to any charges.

When do I pay the service fee?

Service fees are typically charged when you successfully connect with a property owner through our platform. The exact timing and amount are explained during the process.

Can I get a refund if I change my mind?

Service fees are generally non-refundable, but we consider refunds in cases of technical errors or exceptional circumstances. Contact support to discuss your situation.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept major credit cards, debit cards, and mobile money payments common in Kenya. All payments are processed securely through trusted payment providers.
